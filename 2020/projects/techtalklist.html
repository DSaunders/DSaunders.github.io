TechTalkList

A searchable database of thousands of conference talks

Conference Talks are a great way to learn new things and be inspired

There are a lot of talks on YouTube, but it's not a great discovery platform. Talks are spread across multiple channels and playlists, and there's no easy way to find talks from a certain event or speaker.

Likewise, searching for 'React' on YouTube, for example, will return thousands of results; only some of which are relevant

With TechTalkList, I've aggregated thousands of conference talks into a searchable and strucured database; so you can search for a topic and find a talk, and then discover other talks from the same speaker or event.
